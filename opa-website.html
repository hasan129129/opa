About Us

Organization of Peace Assistors (OPA) is a non-profit social service organization based in Bangladesh, dedicated to serving underprivileged communities.

Our Mission

To create a society where every individual has access to basic needs — food, clothing, education, and emergency support — through organized volunteer efforts and community engagement.

Our Vision

A world where compassion drives action, where no one is left behind, and where peace prevails through mutual assistance and understanding.

Our Team

OPA is driven by a passionate team of young volunteers from across Bangladesh who believe in the power of community service.

Our Activities

Explore the various programs and initiatives we run throughout the year.

Iftar Distribution Program

Every Ramadan we distribute iftar meals to fasting individuals who cannot afford their own.

Eid Gift Distribution

We ensure underprivileged families can celebrate Eid with dignity.

Winter Clothing Distribution

During the cold season, we distribute warm clothing and blankets.

Tree Plantation Program

We provide essential support including food, medicine, and shelter assistance.

Awareness Leaflet Distribution Program

Solidarity & Protest Rally (For Palestine)

Boycott Campaign (Israeli Products)

Throughout the day and night, our organization conducted a peaceful campaign to boycott Israeli products, encouraging the use of local alternatives and raising awareness among shopkeepers.
